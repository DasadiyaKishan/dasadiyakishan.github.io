Problem

The founders had three internal tools held together by spreadsheets, a shared Google Drive, and a paid SaaS that didn't quite fit. Onboarding a new team member took half a day and a Loom.

They wanted one product their team would actually open in the morning — something opinionated, fast, and adjustable per workspace.

What I built

Auth and tenants

Workspace-scoped models from day one: every query goes through a tenant guard. Single Laravel app, multi-tenant database, role-based policies on top of Laravel's gate.

Billing surfaces

Plans, seats, invoices, and a reconciliation worker that nightly checks subscriptions against the source of truth. No webhooks-only fragility.

Livewire admin

Reactive tables, modals and inline edits — fast to ship, easy for the next dev to maintain. No SPA build pipeline to babysit.

Outcome

Two major releases in two years, on time. The team grew from solo to five engineers; I led code review, architecture decisions, and the founder relationship.
