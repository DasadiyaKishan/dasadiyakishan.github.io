Asthma Hub

Care plans, symptom tracking and education for asthma patients and the clinicians supporting them — a patient portal and a clinician view.

Role
Solo developer
Surface
Patient + clinician portals
Stack
Laravel 11 · MySQL · AWS
Duration
Ongoing
// Asthma Hub portal screenshot · 16:9

Problem

Patients leave the clinic with a paper plan, lose it, and forget the trigger list. Clinicians lose the thread between visits — there's no shared, low-friction way to follow along.

What I built

Care plans, structured

Plans modelled as data — zones, triggers, medications, escalation steps — not free text. Versioned per visit, owned by the clinician, visible to the patient.

Symptom log

One-tap daily check-in for the patient. The clinician sees the trend, not the entries — saves their attention for the visit.

Two surfaces, one API

Same Laravel app, different policies and views. Patients never see clinician fields and clinicians never have to filter their own dashboard.

Outcome

Live with the founding clinic; expanding through 2026. Built solo, deployed on AWS with a one-command deploy script.
